About

Susan Chelle Dorn is the founder and CEO of Forge Partners, Inc. and the architect of the Stress Test™ and Strength Index™ diagnostic system. With over 20 years of hands-on transformation experience at enterprises including Roche, Genentech, Chevron, State Farm, and Warner Bros., she built Forge Partners on a single conviction: execution failure is organizational before it is operational. The Stress Test™ and Strength Index™ were not theorized in a classroom — they were built in boardrooms, pressure-tested across global transformations affecting hundreds of thousands of people, and refined until they produced a score a leadership team could actually act on.

Her credential stack is the precise combination the market is now demanding — Industrial & Organizational (I-O) psychology, change management, process improvement, project management, executive coaching, and adult education — held simultaneously, not sequentially. This is the foundation of Forge's methodology, and the reason no competitor can replicate it. Chelle serves as the firm's lead diagnostic architect, primary client advisor, and the certifying authority for every practitioner who carries the Forge methodology into an engagement.
